For this project I made a rebrand of Frosted Flakes. I decided to rebrand it with a space theme since Frosted Flakes has a history with space when they were taken on the Apollo 11 mission in 1969 and outer space is something that kids and adults find interesting.

With this project I was tasked with creating a typeface based off the city Honolulu. The typeface I created is inspired by the waves off Waikiki and the relaxing atmosphere typically associated with Honolulu. If I were to redo this project I would make it less sterotypical of what people think of Hawaii.

"The Star" is a sci-fi short story written by Arthur C. Clarke about an astrophysicist who is also a Jesuit priest. He has a crisis of faith when he finds out that the Star of Bethlehem was a star that went supernova and destroyed an entire planet's civilization. For this project I used a limited color palete, shapes, and frames to tell the whole story in a small accordian fold book.
